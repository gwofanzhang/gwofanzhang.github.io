About

I am a Master’s candidate in Medical Physics at Duke University, advised by Prof. Fang‑Fang Yin. My work spans 3D‑printed phantoms for radiotherapy QA and AI‑augmented molecular design for photochemistry/photomedicine. Previously, I trained in the Organic Polymer Functional Materials Lab (SUSTech) and the Electronic Materials & Devices Group (YNU).

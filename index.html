About Me

I am a Master candidate in the Medical Physics at the Duke University. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Fang-Fang Yin. I received my joint Bachelors in Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the Southern University of Science and Technology and Yunnan University.

My primary research interests lie in 3D printing phantom used in radiotherapy and using LLM to predict compound properties. Currently, I am working on Jiangsu Provincial Key Laboratory for Intelligent Diagnosis and Treatment of Lung Cancer. Previously, I have worked on Organic Polymer Functional Materials Laboratory at SUSTech and Electronic Materials and Devices Group at YNU.
